- Marketing language and promotional content

- Every specific numerical value — voltages, temperatures, pressures, weights, ratings
- Every equipment model number and manufacturer name
- Every warning, caution, or note flagged in the original document
- Every operational sequence or procedure described
- Every dependency between systems
